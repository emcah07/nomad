KEGETI GORGE

The Kegeti Gorge is located 70 km from Bishkek and beckons tourists with its breadth and vastness. If some people enjoy hiking, others enjoy cycling. Two waterfalls, spruce forest and Kegeti pass have already managed to fall in love with all visitors. A huge number of people come here just to sit near the waterfalls and relax from the bustle of the city.
The highest point in the gorge is 3200 km above sea level.
The road leading to the gorge is very difficult. For this reason, it is recommended to drive an off-road vehicle with an experienced driver.

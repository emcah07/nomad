NAVEKAT CITY

It is impossible not to remember the ancient Silk Road city, which is located in Chui Oblast, not far from Tokmak.

The Krasnorechenskoye settlement, or Navekat, was a major trading and cultural center of the ancient Turkic Kaganates. Excavations revealed sections of ceramic water pipe, bringing water from the main canal to the houses of wealthy citizens, as well as purification wells.

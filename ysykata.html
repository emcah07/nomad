YSYK-ATA GORGE

The picturesque gorge is located 77 kilometers from Bishkek. The place has gained popularity due to healing hot springs. Many diseases are treated here: arthritis and polyarthritis, gynecological diseases, diseases of the nervous system and digestive organs.
There is a Buddha-physician here - his image on a large stone dates back to the 10th century. People come to him to ask for health.
Nature is also often admired in the gorge. Marble waterfall and Lake Orto-Kel are the most visited points. The Botvei (Byty) Mountain is also noteworthy, about which there is a legend - once Genghis Khan lifted treasures to the top, but could not reach it and hid the riches in the depths of the mountain.
In the gorge grow juniper, mountain ash, rose hips and other shrubs. And trees and alpine meadows add to the beauty of the landscape.

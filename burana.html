BURANA TOWER

Burana Tower was built in X-XI centuries, it is included in the UNESCO List of Tangible Cultural Heritage. It is located 80 kilometers from Bishkek. Earlier its height was 40 meters, but after the earthquake in the XVI century collapsed almost half of it - 19 meters.
The tower is a tapered upward trunk, covered with belts with ornaments. On the south side there is a doorway from which a staircase leading to the observation deck begins. Near the tower there is a rock garden with ancient statues and rock paintings.
